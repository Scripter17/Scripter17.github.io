About me#

Hello! My name is James Wise.
I am an open source developer with a current focus on tools that help ensure the right to privacy.

My best projects#

  • URL Cleaner, explicit non-consent to URL-based spyware such as utm_* query parameters.
  • JREP, a command line tool inspired by GREP and FINDSTR that is designed to be extremely versatile and useful.
  • Minecraft-Hotkeys, a tool for Minecraft to make AFK fishing and some other things easier.
  • AlchemPy, a recreation of the game Little Alchemy made in Python.

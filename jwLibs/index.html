jwBulkDOM

You know what sucks? Having to iterate over a list of elements just to apply one change to them.
Way too often do I find myself doing for (var x=0; x<elems.length, x++){elems[x].style.color="red";} whenever I just need to change a bunch of stuff to be red. Well, with jwBulkDOM.js, you don't have to!

BulkDOM allows you to simply write elems.jwBulkStyle({"color":"red"}); and it will do the iterating for you!

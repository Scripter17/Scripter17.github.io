jwBulkDOM

You know what sucks? Having to iterate over a list of elements just to apply one change to them.
Way too often do I find myself doing something like for (var x=0; x<elems.length, x++){elems[x].style.color="red";} whenever I just need to change a bunch of stuff to be red. Well, with jwBulkDOM.js, you don't have to!

BulkDOM allows you to simply write elems.jwBulkStyle({"color":"red"});, and it will do the iterating for you!

For example, press the button below.

When clicked, it runs document.querySelectorAll("#bulkDOMExample1 > p").jwBulkStyle({"color":"red"}), which makes these two paragraphs red.

While it would undoubtedly be easier/cleaner to simply iterate over the list of items and apply the styling like a normal person, this library is meant for when you need to do a bunch of styling but can't find a clean way to write all of it. Kind of like that last sentence - spaghetti all over the place with no good way to straighten it neatly.
